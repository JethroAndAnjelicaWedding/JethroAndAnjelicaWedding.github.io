Frequently Asked Questions

Do we really need to RSVP?

Yes, it's important! It helps us figure out the headcount for catering and how to set up the seating. Just fill out the form below to tell us your plans.

If you previously RSVP'd 'YES' but are unable to attend, or if you RSVP'd 'NO' but have had a change of circumstances, please let us know as soon as possible so we can try to accommodate any changes.

Can we take pictures and videos to the groom and bride when they walk through the aisle?

Yes you can! Feel free to take pictures and videos as the groom and bride walk down the aisle! Just make sure not to block the aisle or background, ESPECIALLY when the bride is coming through. This helps our professional photographers and videographers get the best shots without any disruptions. Also, please put your phone on silent during the ceremony.

What should I wear?

Let's make our wedding a stylish affair! Dress up in your fanciest formal or semi-formal outfit that goes with our color theme.

The following color motifs are 'Old Rose' , 'Light Old Rose' , 'Gray' , 'Light Gray', 'Light Taupe', and 'Tan'

Please avoid wearing white, beige, and black outfits, slippers, and shorts. Your chic outfits will add to the joy of our special day!

Will parking be available at the reception?

Yes, parking will be available at the reception. There is a designated parking area near the reception for guests' convenience, ensuring easy access and a smooth arrival experience.

Can we sit anywhere at the reception?

We've got seating plans in place for everyone's comfort and preferences. You'll be seated with your family and/or friends.

Can I bring a plus-one (date or other friends) with me?

Our guest list is finalized with a specific number of attendees. Unfortunately, we can't accommodate additional plus-ones or friends beyond those already included in our list. We appreciate your understanding in making our celebration as intimate and enjoyable as possible for everyone.

Can we bring our kids?

If your children are included in the guest list, they are more than welcome to join the celebration. Just make sure they are accounted for in the guest count. We look forward to having the whole family share in our special day!

Do you have any gift preferences?

Your presence is the best gift, and we're grateful for the time and effort you're putting into being there on our special day.
If you wish to give a gift, we would appreciate monetary contributions. Thank you for sharing in our joy!

When can we leave?

We'd love for you to stay until the end and personally thank you for celebrating with us. We hope you can enjoy the entire program. If you need to leave early, feel free to let us know. Your presence means a lot to us!

How can we help the couple have the best time?

To ensure the couple has the best time:
RSVP promptly.
Dress appropriately.
Arrive on time.
Stay until the end.
Use our official hashtag for your social media posts.
Simply enjoy the celebration!

We hope you enjoyed exploring our website. Thank you, and we look forward to seeing you at our wedding!
