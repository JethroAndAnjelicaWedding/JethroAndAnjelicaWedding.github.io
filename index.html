Our Story

It all began with two souls navigating the twists and turns of life until destiny intervened. Little did we know that our individual paths were destined to merge, creating a beautiful tapestry of shared dreams, laughter, and love.

  • Our First Encounter

    2013

    We were not close back then, but I remember when we bumped into each other, she was always smiling. I like that about her.
    - Groom

    We were both student assistants. We crossed paths, but I didn't remember smiling at him, even though I always smile.
    - Bride

  • From Acquaintances to Friends

    2014

    As time went by, our friendship blossomed along with our fellow student assistants. We bonded a few times. One time, we were assigned to the same area. We discovered shared interests, enjoyed countless adventures, and supported each other through the highs and lows. I shared with her my past experiences in love. I didn't realize it back then; I was slowly having feelings for her. What started as a casual acquaintance transformed into a deep, unbreakable bond.
    - Groom

    We became friends along with the other student assistants. Actually, even though we were in the same circle of friends, I didn't know he existed back then. 😂 Not until we were assigned to the same area and became close eventually. We bonded and supported each other, and during this time, I was surprised when he confessed his crush on me. We remained friends, bonded over time, and eventually asked my permission to court me. I didn't know what to feel back then because of the amount of pressure he gave me, but I gave him a chance.
    - Bride

  • The Journey of Love

    2014-2015

    When I decided to express my feelings towards her. I was afraid it would affect our friendship. But I'm glad it didn't. I continued to be friends with her, but was hoping we could be more than that. A month later, I decided to surprise and court her. I was so nervous. But with the help of our friends, I was able to pull it off. Months have passed, she surprised me, and whispered to my ear the magic words. And it marked the day we officially became a couple.
    - Groom



    He bravely shared his feelings, giving me chocolates and notes. I appreciated the gesture. He loves surprising me. It marked a magical chapter with sweet gestures, heartfelt talks, and the joy of discovering each other. The day I finally said "YES" marked the beginning of our official journey as a couple.
    - Bride

  • First Date

    2015

    We decided our first date would be in Lucban, Quezon, and climbed up in "Kamay ni Hesus", where we said our prayers. I surprised her with a DIY Jar of Love that day, only to realize she snuck a gift for me in my bag, which was surprisingly, also a Jar of Love. ❤
    - Groom

    On our first date as a couple, we went to Lucban, Quezon, and climbed up to "Kamay ni Hesus." We prayed and had a little chat up there. I secretly gave him a small gift, a Jar of Love, but was surprised that it was also his gift.
    - Bride

  • In A Relationship

    2015 - 2023

    We were very happy as a couple, but like any other couple, we had struggles and hardships. She moved out of the province and started a new life in Valenzuela. But that didn't stop us from seeing each other. Every once a month, I travel from the province to Valenzuela to visit her. We would go out on dates and explore around Metro Manila, or sometimes just chill out in their home. We made it work for more than 8 years and didn't give up. Through it all, we stood together, never giving up on each other and emerging stronger with every shared experience.
    - Groom

    We shared a special time. Even when I moved to Valenzuela, our love stayed strong. He visited every month, and we explored Metro Manila, creating beautiful memories. Life was tough, but we faced it together. For more than 8 years, our love grew stronger, and every challenge made us closer.
    -Bride

  • The Proposal

    2023

    After being together for more than 8 years, I decided I was ready. I am ready to marry the girl I love. I bought a ring and planned a surprise for her the only way I can: in a room with dim lights, a bouquet of white tulips, balloons saying "Will You Marry Me", candles shaped into a heart, and me in the center, as I made a small speech to her, I got down on one knee and showed her the ring. With only the two of us. It was the most memorable day of our lives, and I knew that all our journey together only led to this path.
    - Groom

    After more than 8 years of love, he surprised me with something big. My exhausted day turned magical when I opened his door, took my hand, and shared heartfelt words. He got down on one knee. With a shaky voice full of emotion, he asked the question that transformed our future. My heart raced as I said "yes," knowing that in that moment, we were stepping into a new chapter, one filled with promises and dreams. It wasn't just a proposal; it was a promise to continue our beautiful story together.
    - Bride

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we really need to RSVP?

Yes, it's important! It helps us figure out the headcount for catering and how to set up the seating. Just fill out the form below to tell us your plans.

If you previously RSVP'd 'YES' but are unable to attend, or if you RSVP'd 'NO' but have had a change of circumstances, please let us know as soon as possible so we can try to accommodate any changes.

Can we take pictures and videos to the groom and bride when they walk through the aisle?

Yes you can! Feel free to take pictures and videos as the groom and bride walk down the aisle! Just make sure not to block the aisle or background, ESPECIALLY when the bride is coming through. This helps our professional photographers and videographers get the best shots without any disruptions. Also, please put your phone on silent during the ceremony.

What should I wear?

Let's make our wedding a stylish affair! Dress up in your fanciest formal or semi-formal outfit that goes with our color theme.

Please avoid wearing white outfits, slippers, and shorts. Your chic outfits will add to the joy of our special day!

Can we sit anywhere at the reception?

We've got seating plans in place for everyone's comfort and preferences. You'll be seated with your family and/or friends.

Can I bring a plus-one (date or other friends) with me?

Our guest list is finalized with a specific number of attendees. Unfortunately, we can't accommodate additional plus-ones or friends beyond those already included in our list. We appreciate your understanding in making our celebration as intimate and enjoyable as possible for everyone.

Can we bring our kids?

If your children are included in the guest list, they are more than welcome to join the celebration. Just make sure they are accounted for in the guest count. We look forward to having the whole family share in our special day!

Do you have any gift preferences?

Your presence is the best gift, and we're grateful for the time and effort you're putting into being there on our special day.
If you wish to give a gift, we would appreciate monetary contributions. Thank you for sharing in our joy!

When can we leave?

We'd love for you to stay until the end and personally thank you for celebrating with us. We hope you can enjoy the entire program. If you need to leave early, feel free to let us know. Your presence means a lot to us!

How can we help the couple have the best time?

To ensure the couple has the best time:
RSVP promptly.
Dress appropriately.
Arrive on time.
Stay until the end.
Simply enjoy the celebration!
