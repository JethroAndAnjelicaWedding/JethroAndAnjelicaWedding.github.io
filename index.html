Our Story

It all began with two souls navigating the twists and turns of life until destiny intervened. Little did we know that our individual paths were destined to merge, creating a beautiful tapestry of shared dreams, laughter, and love.

  • Our First Encounter

    2013

    We were not close back then, but I remember when we bumped into each other, she was always smiling. I like that about her.
    - Groom

    We were both student assistants. We crossed paths, but I didn't remember smiling at him, even though I always smile.
    - Bride

  • From Acquaintances to Friends

    2014

    As time went by, our friendship blossomed along with our fellow student assistants. We bonded a few times. One time, we were assigned to the same area. We discovered shared interests, enjoyed countless adventures, and supported each other through the highs and lows. I shared with her my past experiences in love. I didn't realize it back then; I was slowly having feelings for her. What started as a casual acquaintance transformed into a deep, unbreakable bond.
    - Groom

    We became friends along with the other student assistants. Actually, even though we were in the same circle of friends, I didn't know he existed back then. 😂 Not until we were assigned to the same area and became close eventually. We bonded and supported each other, and during this time, I was surprised when he confessed his crush on me. We remained friends, bonded over time, and eventually asked my permission to court me. I didn't know what to feel back then because of the amount of pressure he gave me, but I gave him a chance.
    - Bride

  • The Journey of Love

    2014-2015

    I decided to express my feelings towards her. I gave her chocolates with a note saying I like her. Our friendship even became stronger then. A month later, I decided to surprise and court her. And then, the day she finally said "YES," we officially became a couple.
    - Groom



    He bravely shared his feelings, giving me chocolates and notes. I appreciated the gesture. He loves surprising me. It marked a magical chapter with sweet gestures, heartfelt talks, and the joy of discovering each other. The day I finally said "YES" marked the beginning of our official journey as a couple.
    - Bride

  • First Date

    2015

    We decided our first date would be in Lucban, Quezon, and climbed up in "Kamay ni Hesus", where we say our prayers. I surprised her with a DIY Jar of Love that day, only to realize she snuck a gift for me in my bag, which was, surprisingly, also a Jar of Love. ❤
    - Groom

    On our first date as a couple, we went to Lucban, Quezon, and climbed up to "Kamay ni Hesus." We prayed and had a little chat up there. I secretly gave him a small gift, a Jar of Love, but was surprised that it was also his gift.
    - Bride

  • In A Relationship

    2015 - 2023

    We were very happy as a couple, but like any other couple, we had struggles and hardships. She moved out of the province and started a new life in Valenzuela. But that didn't stop us from seeing each other. Every once a month, I travel from the province to Valenzuela to visit her. We would go out on dates and explore around Metro Manila, or sometimes just chill out in their home. We made it work for 9 years and didn't give up. Through it all, we stood together, never giving up on each other and emerging stronger with every shared experience.
    - Groom

    We shared a special time. Even when I moved to Valenzuela, our love stayed strong. He visited every month, and we explored Metro Manila, creating beautiful memories. Life was tough, but we faced it together. For 9 years, our love grew stronger, and every challenge made us closer.
    -Bride

  • The Proposal

    2023

    After being together for 8 years, I decided I was ready. I am ready to marry the girl I love. I decided to buy her the only thing that was suitable for her. A diamond ring. And surprise her the only way I can: in a room with dim lights, a bouquet of white tulips, balloons saying "Will You Marry Me", candles shaped into a heart, and me in the center, as I made a small speech to her, I got down on one knee and showed her the ring. With only the two of us. It was the most memorable day of our lives, and I knew that all our journey together only led to this path.
    - Groom

    After 8 years of love, he surprised me with something big. My exhausted day turned magical when I opened his door, took my hand, and shared heartfelt words. He got down on one knee. With a shaky voice full of emotion, he asked the question that transformed our future. My heart raced as I said "yes," knowing that in that moment, we were stepping into a new chapter, one filled with promises and dreams. It wasn't just a proposal; it was a promise to continue our beautiful story together.
    - Bride

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we really need to RSVP?

Yes. This will help us finalize the headcount for catering and seating arrangement. Please fill up the form above to notify us. I RSVP'd YES but can't come or RSVP'd NO but has a change of circumstances: Please let us know ASAP so we can try to accomodate for any changes.

Can we take pictures and videos to the groom and bride when they walk through the aisle?

Yes you can! As long as you keep the aisle and background clear while the groom and ESPECIALLY the bride walk through the aisle. This will help our official Photographers and Videographers to capture this moment without interuption. Also, during the ceremony, please put your phone on silent.

What should I wear?

Make our wedding a fun excuse to dress up and be a fab! Wear your best formal/semi-formal attire that will match our color theme.

No wearing of white/black attire, slippers, and shorts, please.

Can we sit anywhere at the reception?

We plan to arrange the seating arrangement for everyone's convenience and preferences. You will be seated along with family and friends.

Can I bring a plus-one (date or other friends) with me?

We can only accomodate a limited number of guests which are only included in our guest list.

Can we bring our kids?

Kids will also be included in our guest list so if they are included, they can come along.

Do you have any gift preferences?

We are grateful for the time and effort you'll be spending just to be there on our special day. We'd be thankful to receive monetary gifts!

When can we leave?

We would like to personally thank you for joining us to celebrate, hopefully you can finish the program.
